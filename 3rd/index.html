Zamalek Sporting Club

Zamalek Sports Club, or as it is known as Zamalek Club for short, is an Egyptian professional sports club that plays in the Egyptian League, and it is one of seven clubs that have not been relegated to the second division, and it is one of the clubs that have never been relegated from the top of Egyptian football. Since the start of the league alongside Al-Ahly.Zamalek established itself as the second major force in Egyptian football during the 1920s, as it became the second Egyptian team to win titles.
